Learning & Development (L&D) is an integral part of all employee retention and engagement strategies in a modern organization. Since classroom training is neither convenient nor does it provide economies of scale, e-learning has emerged as the most reliable mechanism to impart comprehensive, high-quality, anytime, anywhere learning to a large group of participants.

iTransmaster, which is at the forefront of Content Development Solutions in the Indian market, offers a suite of e-learning solutions tailored to the specific needs of your organization or target segment. By partnering with iTransmaster, you can create or curate unique content and position yourself as subject matter experts in your desired field of choice.

INSTRUCTIONAL DESIGN (ID)

Instructional Design offers end-to-end and interactive learning experience about a subject. It allows learners to interact with content, explore information, take decisions, and construct knowledge, in order to generate effective learning. This is enabled through rich content in the form of storyboards, fables, narratives, metaphors, scenarios, case studies and private opinions that enhance the learning value over plain, textual information.

iTransmaster can provide end-to-end support for ID which includes Content Research, Content Design, storyboard review, and editing and proofreading of final output.

K-12 Education

We provide curriculum-based Educational content for Kindergarten to +12 (senior secondary) for all the standard systems of syllabus available in India. The course material is created for all modes of learning such as Classroom, online and self-learning and is rich in Activity-based learning.

HIGHER/TECHNICAL EDUCATION

We provide learning content for Pre-university, Graduation and Post-graduation levels covering all science, technical, language, humanities and other subjects. The course material is created for all modes of learning such as Classroom, online and self-learning and is rich in Activity-based learning.

CORPORATE TRAINING

Our e-Learning modules can be used to address internal training requirements at any IT/ITES, Consulting, Manufacturing or Engineering Services organization. We support custom content development for either an Instructor-led training or web-based self-learning modules.

TRAINING FOR MANUFACTURING INDUSTRY

Our e-Learning modules can be used to equip and enable shop-floor staff with adequate self-help, quick reference guides, ready reckoners, etc. We can impart technical know-how and training through Virtual labs environments and on-the-job manuals, troubleshooting aids, etc.

TRAINING DOCUMENTATION

For the Engineering & Manufacturing sectors as well as for Business & Corporate Training, we offer comprehensive Training Documentation. We can analyze, audit and develop training courseware which includes storyboarding, review and end-to-end packaged development.

COURSEWARE DESIGN AND DEVELOPMENT

For K-12 Education, Higher and Technical Education as well as for Corporate Training, we can design and develop full-fledged courseware which includes storyboard, animations & videos, Activity Design (DIY or Field Action Plans), and Assessment Design (MCQs or Questionnaires).

CONTENT DESIGN AND DEVELOPMENT

For organizations or individuals who would like to develop Content, we can help with Syllabus Mapping, Subject Matter Expertise support, Teaching Aids (Instructional Plan, Speaker Notes) and Student Aids (Take-away notes and ready reckoners).
