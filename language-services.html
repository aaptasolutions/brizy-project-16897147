TRANSLATION

One of the key considerations in todays commercial world is managing the challenges of Globalization.

Today the ability to communicate in a multitude of languages has become essential in the world of business. Translation plays a critical role. With current market trends and the need for efficient and quality translation growing by the day, iTransmaster offers a plethora of solutions.

Proofreading, Localization and voice-over solutions in numerous languages and dialects from across the world - these are just some of the services we offer.

Our speciality lies in our commitment driven by experienced and dedicated project managers with impeccable skill sets. By using Native Translators, we ensure that the quality of our work is unparalleled. Having started our translation agency with the primary focus being the Indian subcontinent, our quality of work, along with our professionalism has quickly helped us become the Vendor of Choice for Clients across the globe.

We have a network of over 3000 linguists across the globe, the best in the industry. They are native/non-native speakers who have been born and educated in the country of the target language and are specialised in their respective fields. They are selected after careful evaluation and testing. Drawing upon their expertise and specialisation, we are capable of undertaking projects ranging from simple to highly complex and technical fields.

TRANSCRIPTION

As part of our Linguistic services we offer an exhaustive package of transcription services. Backed by a team of professional transcriptionists, we can handle multiple languages, accents and domains without deviation from the source.

We offer high-quality and cost-effective voice transcription services and we believe it is not just being linguistically proficient. Our transcriptionists are multi-skilled and have domain-specific knowledge which helps them handle the finer subtleties involved. Flooded with a number of national and international accents and dialects, we are highly research oriented to ensure the jargon and terminology used is accurate.

VOICE OVER

Professional Support: We offer professional voice-over services and a wide range of choices with professional voice-over artists. Our team has the ability to judge on the right combination of linguistic and vocal parameters which include language, dialect, gender, accent, pitch and emotion.

SUB-TITLING

Today subtitling services are used across animation, entertainment, education, product demos, etc. This has optimized results and helped audiences truly relate.

iTransmaster offers subtitling services which includes dialogue adaptation from an existing language into a specified language. To create appropriate subtitles, our team of subtitling linguists ensure the dialogues meaning and length is maintained for easy, quick and accurate readability that fits within the time of the scene. At iTransmaster, we believe the proposition for good subtitling is simple - just be clear, concise and consistent.
