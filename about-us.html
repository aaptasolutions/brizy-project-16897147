Building the Corporation

iTransmaster Solutions Pvt. Ltd. (ITS), is more than just a company. We are a group comprised of individuals from high profile environments who came together via a meeting of minds, put our thoughts on the table and decided to build a corporation that makes a difference. With an ambition to give this industry a new dimension, we look at innovative and creative ways to capture markets.

Harmonizing the business

We are a multifaceted Business Service Provider with offices in Bangalore and Mumbai. We engage in planning language services and documentation strategies that can realign existing content by evolving a strategic plan that enhances brand image all at highly competitive prices.

Creating the niche

Our business is to provide niche language services and documentation services across industries. Our vision is to create content which is both technical and web-based, yet engaging enough to have a high-impact for an enhanced business image. We develop product documentation, technical support, and training materials. Supported by the best talent in the industry, our processes are ISO compliant with the highest caliber, and unparalleled client satisfaction.

The Asia-Pacific region is one of the fastest growing areas for the Translation and Localization industry. Our extensive experience over decades in this Industry, along with axiomatic results, foresee the APAC as the Industry leaders in Translation and Localization services for the foreseeable future. The entire knowledge based service and solutions Industry has shown exponential growth over the past years. Starting with the multiple modalities of manufacturing techniques worldwide, today we see that the Translation and Content Services Domain has become an integral part of every business domain. From Engineering to Healthcare, Pharma to BFSI, IT/ITES to Education, there is not one Industry today that does not require Linguistic Services. As Globalization continues to reinforce the phrase One Voice many Languages - iTransmasters Linguistic Services offer an incredible opportunity to Businesses and Individuals alike, who are looking to reach out Globally but with a Local Voice.
