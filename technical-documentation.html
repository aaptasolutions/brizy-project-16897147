iTransmaster is from the school of thought that technical writing is not just about learning and writing about new technologies, domains, product suites and processes. Its how you can set business direction, align and motivate others and deliver results.

Technical writing is considered to be a part of technical communication. It is used in fields as diverse as computer hardware and software, chemistry, aerospace industry, robotics, finance, consumer electronics and biotechnology.

Technical documentation describes functionality and architecture of a technical product under development or in use.

Technical documentation may vary - Product definition and specification, design, manufacturing, quality assurance, product liability, product presentation; description of features, functions and interfaces; intended, safe and correct use; service and repair of a technical product.
